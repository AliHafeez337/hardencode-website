Privacy

This site does not run its own tracking scripts or advertising pixels. Booking a call uses Koalendar, and Koalendar handles the name, email, and time you provide for that booking. I never sell your information to anyone, and I never share it beyond what is needed to have the conversation you asked for.

This applies whether you are covered by the GDPR, the CCPA, or no specific privacy law at all. If you want your information removed at any point, email me and I will handle it directly, no automated runaround.
